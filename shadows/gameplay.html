Gameplay Mechanics

Exploration

Navigate through a vast post-apocalyptic world filled with hidden secrets and dangerous territories.

Quest Completion

Complete various quests to progress the story and unlock new abilities and items.

Moral Decision-Making

Make choices that affect the storyline and the fate of the characters you encounter.

Stealth

Utilize stealth tactics to avoid detection and, navigate through hostile environments undetected.

Changing Ideologies

Experience dynamic character development as Nathan's ideologies evolve based on his actions and decisions throughout the game.
