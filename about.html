About Me

Tyler Huffaker - Who am I?

Hello! My name is Tyler Huffaker, and I'm a passionate video game developer. At just 20 years old, I have a beautiful wife and three amazing children. I've had a diverse journey so far, having served in the army and currently working for KUB Fiber in Knoxville, Tennessee, where I specialize in fiber optic installations.

Reigniting My Passion for Coding

While my work in the army and my current job have been fulfilling, I've always had a deep passion for coding and video game development. Now, I'm excited to embark on a new chapter of my life, where I'm reigniting that passion by creating and designing my very first official large video game.

My Vision

With this project, I aim to bring my unique experiences and perspectives into the world of video game development. My goal is to create a game that not only entertains but also tells a meaningful story, combining immersive gameplay with thought-provoking themes.

Join Me on this Journey

I invite you to join me on this exciting journey as I develop my very first official large video game. Stay updated with the latest news and progress by checking up on the projects page of my website.
